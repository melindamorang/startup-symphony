The following is a suggested list of things to do, in order, to start your orchestra.

  1. Determine your vision and goals
  2. Find a conductor
  3. Find rehearsal and performance space
  4. Start a website
  5. Recruit musicians
  6. Schedule rehearsals and concerts
  7. Acquire some sheet music
  8. Start rehearsing!
  9. Build your orchestra's administrative leadership
  10. Open a bank account
  11. Incorporate as a business at the state level
  12. Write bylaws
  13. Apply for 501(c)(3) nonprofit status

Many of these steps include substeps, so make sure to read carefully and follow the links.
